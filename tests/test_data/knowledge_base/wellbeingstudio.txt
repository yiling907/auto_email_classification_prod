## Wellbeing Studio

#### Digital Gym & Wellbeing Series
Laya healthcare members aged 16+ have access, at no additional cost, to the Digital Gym and Wellbeing Series.
As Ireland's leading Health & Wellbeing provider, we're able to access the largest panel of health and wellbeing experts to offer an incredible weekly schedule of classes and seminars.
To make it even better, all classes and seminars are available live and on-demand. So, like thousands of our members so far, you can watch them back and take part at a time that suits you.
Not a laya healthcare member? Switch today and get access to your Wellbeing Studio.

#### Digital Gym
Digital Gym gives members access to live & on demand fitness classes, with a weekly schedule for them to choose from. All classes and training plans are delivered by experts who will demonstrate safe and maintainable ways to tackle fitness at home.

#### Wellbeing Series
Members can use our online platform to access a weekly topical wellbeing seminar based on published wellbeing calendars, & some additional themes of our own. The seminars will be presented by our health & wellbeing experts or special guest presenters.
