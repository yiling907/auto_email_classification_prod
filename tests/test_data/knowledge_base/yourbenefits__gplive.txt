#### How does GPLive work?
GPlive is an online video consultation service which allows you to speak face to face with a GP through your phone, tablet, laptop or desktop. GPlive GPs will be able to provide clinical advice and guidance and, if safe and suitable for you, issue a private prescription. They can also refer you for minor diagnostic tests and, if necessary, refer you for the most appropriate further treatment.

#### Specialist consultations via CareOnCall
From 1st April, laya healthcare is expanding the breadth of services available through CareOnCall, which already delivers over 100,000 digital consultations to members each year. Members can now access new digital specialist GP consultations, led by GPs with advanced qualifications in specific areas. A co-payment applies for these services, with details available when you log into CareOnCall.ie. At launch, two specialist services are available: a Menopause service for women aged 41–69, offering comprehensive assessment and management of perimenopause and menopause symptoms—including hormonal support, HRT using body-identical hormones, testosterone therapy where appropriate, and lifestyle guidance; and a Dermatology service providing rapid access to diagnosis and treatment for common skin, hair, and scalp conditions such as eczema, psoriasis, acne, infections, and mole assessments. These services aim to offer faster, more personalised care while reducing the need for lengthy specialist wait times.

#### Need a medical cert, sick note or Consultant referral?
With GPlive, not only can you get diagnosed and treated for range of conditions from coughs, colds, chest infections and even sports injuries, if required, our GP’s can also refer you to a Consultant and even issue you with a medical cert should they feel you require some time off work to recover from your illness or injury.
