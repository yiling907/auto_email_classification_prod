POLICY COVERAGE INFORMATION - InsureMail AI Insurance

MEDICAL COVERAGE:

Diagnostic Services:
- X-rays, MRI, CT scans: Covered at 80% after deductible
- Lab tests: Covered at 90%
- Annual physical exam: Covered at 100% (no deductible)

Specialist Care:
- Specialist consultations: Covered at 70% after deductible
- Follow-up visits: Same as initial consultation
- Referral required for coverage (except emergencies)

DENTAL COVERAGE:

Basic Dental Services:
- Cleanings (2 per year): Covered at 100%
- Fillings: Covered at 80%
- X-rays: Covered at 90%

Major Dental Services:
- Root canals: Covered at 60% after $500 dental deductible
- Crowns: Covered at 50%
- Bridges: Covered at 50%
- Dentures: Covered at 50%

DEDUCTIBLES:
- Individual medical deductible: $1,500 per year
- Family medical deductible: $3,000 per year
- Individual dental deductible: $500 per year

OUT-OF-POCKET MAXIMUM:
- Individual: $6,000 per year
- Family: $12,000 per year

COVERAGE EXCLUSIONS:
- Cosmetic procedures
- Experimental treatments
- Services not medically necessary
- Treatment from non-network providers (unless emergency)

For detailed coverage questions, refer to your policy documents or contact customer service.
