## Digital Health Hub Benefits
Welcome to the Digital Health Hub, a suite of digital services available exclusively to Laya healthcare members*. Here are the benefits you can access:
  - Video consultations with a GP (for members aged 17+)
  - Consultations with a physiotherapist (for members aged 17+)
  - 1:1 video consultations with health and wellbeing experts covering parenting, financial, and wellbeing advice (for members aged 17+)
  - Unlimited personal consultations with a dietician (for members aged 17+)
  - Healthcoach video consultations (for members aged 18+)
Please note that members under 17 can access all benefits except for Healthcoach, but they must be booked by their parent or guardian who must attend the appointment with them.
Our members have had a fantastic experience with these benefits, and we delivered over 110,000 digital health consultations in 2022. For details of cover, please refer to your table of benefits.

#### GPlive
Connect with an Irish registered GP from anywhere with GPlive's online video consultations. Get medical advice, prescriptions, and referrals when necessary for non-emergency situations that don't require a physical exam. Book your appointment now with GPlive for quick and convenient healthcare.
Find out more

#### Digital Healthcoach
Looking to achieve your health goals and prevent future health issues? Our Healthcoach service offers personalised one-to-one coaching with a qualified expert to design an individual programme tailored to your unique needs. Take control of your health, succeed, and thrive with the right support.
Find out more

#### Digital Physiotherapist
Get access to a digital physiotherapist who can help assess a range of conditions and provide a treatment plan. Our expert consultation and advice will help you alleviate pain or discomfort you're experiencing.
Find out more

#### Health & Wellbeing Experts
Get access to a range of Health & Wellbeing experts, including digital dieticians, Qualified Financial advisors, parenting experts, and more. Speak with an expert to organize a tailored nutrition plan or get professional advice on parenting, budgeting, financial planning, and other issues. Our team of experts is always available to support you.
Find out more

#### Laya Healthcare Concierge
Get health advice and guidance from Laya Healthcare Concierge. Our experienced nurses, including midwife and pediatric nurses, are supported by a medical advice panel. From maternity care to life-changing diagnoses and recovery times, our Concierge service has got you covered. Ask us anything and make informed decisions about your health.
Find out more

#### Online Prescription Service
Need a prescription but can't make it to the doctor? No problem! With our online prescription service, you can get the medication you need in just 3 easy steps. First, fill out our secure online questionnaire. Next, our Irish-registered doctors will review your answers to ensure the medication is safe and appropriate for you. Finally, your prescription will be sent directly to the pharmacy of your choice. It's fast, safe, and secure!
Find out more

#### What are the benefits available through the Digital health Hub?
While fair usage applies in terms of how these benefits can be accessed members have access to:
  - GPlive, members aged 17+can have a video consultations with a GP 7 days a week
  - Digital consultations with a Physiotherapist for members aged 17+
  - Digital consultations with Health & Wellbeing experts covering parenting, financial and wellbeing advice for members aged 17+
  - Digital consultations with an expert Dietician – for members aged 17+
  - Online prescription service for members aged 17+
  - Members under the age of 17 can also access the above services, the appointment must be booked on their behalf by a parent/guardian who must also attend the appointment with the member.
  - Digital Healthcoach video, for members aged 18+
