## Cardiac health

#### Prevention and hospital care
Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death in Ireland and it can strike at any age.
With laya healthcare, you have access to Centre of Excellence cardiac units and 100+ public, private and high-tech hospitals around Ireland, along with over 98% of Consultants.
80% of premature heart disease and stroke is preventable*. We also ensure you have access to sophisticated cardiac screening. This gives you a clear picture of your heart health over time and minimises your risk of developing heart disease.

#### Screening, tests and prevention
At laya healthcare we’re always a beat ahead so our members can be too. We invest in more comprehensive cardiac screening benefits than any other health insurer in Ireland, because heart disease is preventable.
HeartBeat Cardiac Screening
Monitor your heart health with our thorough cardiac screening service, which is available to every laya healthcare member over the age of 12 every two years. It includes a physical exam, ECG and Consultant review. Regular health checks and screenings are more important than ever and now you can book an appointment online through your Member Area or on the Laya app.
Frequently Asked Questions - HeartBeat Cardiac Screening
Cardiac tests
Our members get access to diagnostic tests in public and private hospitals, as well as specialist cardiac units across Ireland.
Please note approximately 6% of participants may be recommended to attend for further follow up cardiac investigations, based on the results of their screening. These are not covered as part of the screening but may be claimable under your everyday medical expenses benefit, depending on your scheme. Please check your Member Area for more details.
Healthcoach
We empower you to take control of your health, with a one-to-one health check with a qualified Healthcoach. They’ll give you a personalised fitness, nutrition and wellbeing programme. (For members aged over 18.)
Executive Health Checks
Your laya healthcare scheme may partly cover the cost of an Executive Health Check in a private hospital once every two years.
Check your cover in your Member Area .

#### Heart illness treatment and support
When it comes to cardiac cover, with laya healthcare you’re in control . Choose the level of cover that’s right for you when it comes to cardiac care in public and private hospitals. Some schemes include the option of private or semi-private accommodation and your choice of specialist cardiac Consultant.
As a laya healthcare member, your scheme can also include:
  - Rapid access to diagnostics
  - Cover for a range of treatment options, including surgery in public or private hospitals across Ireland
  - Access to alternative treatments such as acupuncture, dietetics and osteopathy.
Check your cover in your Member Area .

#### Recovery
Being diagnosed with a heart problem is frightening and can be life-changing. Heart disease has no cure, but we’ll give you supports and services to help you live with the condition and improve your heart health. These include:
  - Money back on GP visits and physio appointments
  - 24/7 access to doctors and nurses with GPline and Nurseline
  - 24/7 Mental Wellbeing Support from qualified counsellors
  - Book an online appointment with a Chartered Physiotherapist through Member Area.
  - Access to a qualified Dietician online, to improve your diet. You can book this and a wide variety of services through your Member Area.
  - A personalised fitness, nutrition and wellbeing programme by your dedicated Healthcoach (over 18s)
  - Our 24/7 Mental Wellbeing Support Programme if you need help managing stress and anxiety
Check your cover in your Member Area .
