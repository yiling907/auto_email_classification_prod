## CareOnCall

#### No matter what time of the day or day of the week, through our CareOnCall service we are here to Look After You Always.
CareOnCall offers online GP consultations seven days a week, a home prescription service and unlimited phone access to GPs, nurses and physios. The only service of its kind on the market, CareOnCall is available to all laya healthcare members. To review whether some of these benefits are available on your scheme now, log in to your Member Area.

#### What makes CareOnCall unique?
  - GPlive, our online GP consultation is available to suit busy schedules, with all GPs based, practicing and registered in Ireland
  - Online prescription service offers convenient and simple access, and delivery to your door
  - Our dedicated smartphone app and responsive website give you access on the go
  - GPline and Nurseline are available 24/7 for immediate access to advice
  - Home testing kits for a range of medical tests
