## What we treat

### Ireland’s faster urgent care - be seen within 60 minutes, 365 days a year
If you’re struck by sudden back pain, sustain a sports injury or have an ear infection or UTI, our expert medical team can treat you at a Laya Health and Wellbeing Clinic. Our clinics are open 10am - 10pm, 365 days a year, to everyone over the age of 12 months, and you won’t wait any more than an hour to be seen.
Our members can also avail of a range of wellbeing services – things like HeartBeat cardiac screening and consultations with a Healthcoach.
  - Wounds (including stitches)
  - Sports injuries
  - Fractures (full digital x-ray on-site)
  - Minor burns
  - Back pain
  - Nausea, diarrhoea and vomiting
  - Urinary tract infections
  - Skin infections
  - Ear infections
  - Book a video consultation
  - Pregnancy-related issues
  - Chest pain or irregular heartbeat
  - Unconsciousness, seizures, stroke
  - Mental health issues
  - Overdose, poisoning or attempted suicide
  - Severe abdominal pain
  - Severe breathing difficulties
  - Vision problems or severe head injuries
  - Vomiting requiring IV fluids
  - Severe headache
  - Children under the age of 12 months
Please go to your nearest hospital or emergency department if you or the person you are looking after are suffering from any of these issues.
Patients who require medical treatment for a sore throat, runny nose, headache or fever can avail of the walk-in service once they present a negative antigen test on arrival. Video consultations for appropriate minor illnesses including kidney infections and skin and eye issues are available with one of our Clinic Doctors.

### Our wellbeing services

#### Healthcoach
Available to all members aged 18+
Make the most of our expert preventative healthcare service. Get an individual assessment and track your progress with your customised plan in our Healthcoach app.
  - One-to-one appointment
  - Personalised assessment
  - Tailored plan and tracking
  - Support and inspiration

#### HeartBeat Cardiac Screening
Available to all members aged 12+
Don’t wait until you’re sick to look after your heart. Our HeartBeat screening service is designed to reduce your risk of heart disease and sudden adult death syndrome.
  - Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  - Physical examination
  - Blood pressure and BMI check
  - Cardiologist review

#### Out of hours services
Get expert medical advice when you need it.
Any time of the day or night, we make sure you can get professional advice from a nurse, doctor or fully qualified counsellor through our phone lines.
  - 24/7 GPline
  - 24/7 Nurseline
  - 24/7 Mental Wellbeing Support Programme
