## Boost your health with Healthcoach

#### Book your appointment today
Prevention is always better than cure. That’s why we offer you expert preventative healthcare so you can stay healthy and safeguard your future.
As your health goals are unique to you, we will give you personalised one-to-one coaching with a qualified Healthcoach. We want you to have the right support to take control, succeed and thrive.
The Healthcoach will design a fitness, nutrition and wellbeing programme to help you achieve your health, fitness and wellbeing goals. The Healthcoach will also refer into other Laya services such as dietetics, physiotherapy, financial advisor, parenting coach, wellbeing studio, pregnancy and early childhood nutrition, HeartBeat cardiac screening, and so on to further support you in achieving your goals.
Whatever fitness, nutrition, wellbeing or other health goals you want to reach, your expert Healthcoach will work with you to design an individual programme.
And you’ll always have support on your journey to better health – with online Healthcoach reviews and follow-up sessions.

#### When can I see a Healthcoach?
Every member over the age of 18 is entitled to an in-person consultation with a Healthcoach once every two years. Book your session now .
We’re providing unlimited digital Healthcoach sessions so all our members get the tailored motivation they need. (Fair use applies.)

#### How does Healthcoach work?

#### One-to-one appointment
  - Book a digital or in-person consultation with a qualified Healthcoach through Member Area

#### Your personalised assessment
  - Your Healthcoach will carry out a personalised health assessment using a number of baseline metrics and measurements
  - Discuss your health and wellbeing goals

#### Tailored plan
  - Shape a personalised plan along with your insights and goals
  - Tailor the programme to your needs and goals
  - Form SMART goals with your Healthcoach and plan how to work towards achieving these

#### Support and inspiration
  - After your Healthcoach appointment, you will receive a detailed action plan from your dedicated Healthcoach
  - Receive a member pack which highlights some of our health and wellbeing benefits and services that could support you in achieving your goals
  - Book your digital follow-up session

#### What is Healthcoach by Laya Healthcare?
Healthcoach is a unique benefit exclusively available to Laya Healthcare members, empowering you to live and feel better with a consultation with a qualified Healthcoach.
During your consultation, your Healthcoach will carry out a personalised health assessment using baseline measurements which will shape your personalised plan along with your insights and goals.
The Healthcoach will give you a personalised tour of your Digital Hub where you can access material on fitness, nutrition and wellbeing content and information .

#### Who can access Healthcoach?
The personalised Healthcoach service is available to all Laya Healthcare members over the age of 18.

#### Is there a cost for Healthcoach?
Every Laya Healthcare member over the age of 18 is entitled to an in-person consultation with a Healthcoach once every two years at no extra cost.
All members can also access unlimited digital Healthcoach sessions until the end of 2024. (Fair use applies.)

#### How do I access my personalised Healthcoach programme?
After your in-person or digital consultation, your dedicated Healthcoach will send your personalised Healthcoach programme to your email.
