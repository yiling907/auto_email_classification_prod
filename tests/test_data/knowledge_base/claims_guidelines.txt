CLAIMS PROCESSING GUIDELINES - InsureMail AI Insurance

Standard Claims Processing Timeline:
- Claims are typically processed within 14 business days of receipt
- Complex claims may require up to 30 business days
- Missing documentation will delay processing

Required Documentation for Medical Claims:
1. Completed claim form
2. Itemized bill from healthcare provider
3. Medical records or procedure notes
4. Proof of payment (if already paid)
5. Policy number and member ID

Claims Status Updates:
- Members can check claim status online at insuremailai.com/claims
- Email notifications sent at key processing stages:
  * Receipt confirmation
  * Under review
  * Additional information needed
  * Processed (approved or denied)

Pre-Authorization Requirements:
- Required for procedures over $5,000
- Must be obtained at least 5 business days before procedure
- Emergency procedures are exempt
- Pre-auth requests: preauth@insuremailai.com

Claims Appeals:
- Members have 60 days to appeal a denied claim
- Appeals must include:
  * Original claim number
  * Reason for appeal
  * Supporting medical documentation
- Appeal processing: 30 business days

For urgent claim inquiries, contact: 1-800-CLAIMS-1
