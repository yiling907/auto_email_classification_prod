## Laya Healthcare, Part of AXA.
We’re excited to announce that laya healthcare has now joined AXA Ireland. Working together, we can now help our members protect what matters most by offering an extensive range of insurance products and services.
For the past 25 years, AXA has been protecting homes and cars throughout the country. They are one of Ireland’s largest and oldest insurance companies, and we are delighted to come together with them.
This is the perfect next step in laya healthcare’s journey to ensure our members’ continued health. AXA is the largest health insurance provider in Europe. Health and wellbeing are at the heart of their business, and we’re delighted to join a global leader and work towards a healthier future for all.

## Looking after you, always
AXA is an established leader in insurance with over 93 million customers in more than 51 countries worldwide. Take a look at AXA’s insurance offerings below.

#### Car Insurance
Insure your car with an AXA comprehensive or third party, fire & theft policy and tailor your policy with optional extras to suit your needs.

#### Home Insurance
Homeowners can choose to insure their house, contents, or both. Protect your home against fire, theft, storms and more.

#### Farm Insurance
AXA farm insurance covers your farm vehicles, properties, livestock, outbuildings and public liability under one policy.

#### Van Insurance
Whether you use your van for business or pleasure, you will need to insure it to be protected. Choose between a standard or premier policy.

#### Taxi Insurance
AXA offers tailored taxi insurance to help you get your taxi on the road with the cover you need to stay protected.

#### Small Business Insurance
This comprehensive coverage includes public liability cover, employer liability cover and more. It is ideal for retail owners, offices and surgeries.
