## Healthcare Concierge

#### Call on expert advice and support

#### Concerned about a new diagnosis? Frustrated by medical jargon? Unsure what your next step should be?
When you have healthcare questions, your Laya Healthcare Concierge is here for you, offering understanding, answers and guidance.
Healthcare Concierge - the first of its kind in Ireland - is a personal service for laya healthcare members. A team of experienced nurses can guide you every step of the way through Ireland's complex healthcare system.
Your Healthcare Concierge give you and your loved ones support & reassurance when you need it most, with information tailored to your diagnosis and treatment.

#### Looking after you always

## Expertise on call
Your Healthcare Concierge is part of a team of nurses – including midwives and paediatric nurses – supported by a medical advice panel.
They have decades of clinical experience navigating Ireland’s healthcare system and supporting patients every step of the way.
Your Healthcare Concierge can offer you:
  - help understanding your own, or a family member's diagnosis
  - guidance on assessing public and private healthcare services in Ireland
  - pre- post-natal advice from a midwife
  - clarity when you’re dealing with complex medical terminology and jargon
  - faster access to certain procedures and scans in select hospitals
  - an understanding of what you can expect when admitted to hospital and when discharged
Call 021 202 2893

  - Where can I find the right consultant?

  - What’s going to happen when I leave hospital?

#### Healthcare Concierge

## How does it work
It's simple. When you have any healthcare-related queries, contact your Laya Healthcare Concierge team directly for answers and advice.
1. Call or email us any time 9am - 5pm, Monday through Friday on 021 202 2893 or email concierge@layahealthcare.ie
2. Get a response on the spot from an experienced Healthcare Concierge.
3. Get further details in response to any complex questions when your Healthcare Concierge emails or calls you back.
4. Get a follow-up advice summary, and any supporting documents you need.
Healthcare Concierge is here to help you with any healthcare related questions you might have on your mind.
