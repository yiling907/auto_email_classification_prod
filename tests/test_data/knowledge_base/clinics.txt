### What we treat

#### Don’t wait hours at a hospital A&E. Come to our state-of-the-art Consultant-led clinics for the urgent care you need.
Sprained ankle or burned hand? When you come to a Laya Health and Wellbeing Clinic, you’ll be seen within an hour by our expert medical team.
Access faster walk-in urgent care for minor injuries and illnesses 10am-10pm, 365 days a year.

### Your clinic cover

#### We offer accessible healthcare to everyone, so anyone over 12 months can get urgent medical care at our Consultant-led clinics.
Our clinics are open to both Laya members and non-members. If you're a member, a 'per visit' excess may apply. Excess (amount owed) varies depending on your plan. To find out the exact amount, please check your coverage details in the Member Area of your app.
