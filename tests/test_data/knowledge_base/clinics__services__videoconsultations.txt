## Video Consultations

### Laya Health and Wellbeing Clinics
Our clinics are open on a walk-in basis 10am-10pm, 365 days a year.
We also offer video consultations for minor illnesses including kidney infections and skin and eye issues with one of our Clinic Doctors for patients aged 12+ months. Video Consultations can usually be booked within 2 hours. To book a Laya Health and Wellbeing Clinic video consultation, call our Clinic on 0818 933 030

#### To Book A Video Consultation
Call 0818 933 030
Video consultations can be used for:
  - Clinical advice on appropriate minor illnesses including kidney infections, skin and eye issues.
  - Prescription service to treat acute symptoms; antibiotics, painkillers, steroid creams etc if appropriate.
  - Sick notes if appropriate.
Video consultations should not be used for:
  - Any illness or symptoms related to Covid-19 (coronavirus) (please contact your own GP or the HSE directly on 1850 24 1850 who will direct you on next steps appropriate to your symptoms and situation)
  - Any hands-on examination and treatment
  - Any emergency treatment e.g. chest pain, stroke symptoms or bone fractures
  - Any pregnancy related issues
  - Any controlled medications e.g. Morphine prescriptions
  - Any mental health issues.
  - Any neurological issue
  - Please note, we aim to provide the most appropriate treatment for our patients, therefore some minor illnesses may not be suitable for a video consultation.
Costs for video consultations:
  - Laya healthcare members can access unlimited Laya Health and Wellbeing Clinic video consultations as per the QuickCare benefit in your scheme.
  - If you are not a laya healthcare member, the cost of consultation is €60. This is payable by credit card in advance with an additional charge of €15 for prescriptions.

Get expert medical advice when you need it.

  - 24/7 Nurseline
  - 24/7 Mental Wellbeing Support Programme
