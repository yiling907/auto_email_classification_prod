## Nurseline

#### 24/7 Phone Access to a Nurse
When we say, “Looking after you always”, that also means in the middle of the night when your child has woken up unwell.
Just call our Nurseline and we’ll be there to give you advice and peace of mind. Nurseline is run by qualified nurses who have a range of clinical experience.
You’ll find a sympathetic ear and answers to any health questions or concerns. The service is completely confidential and you can call at any time, day or night.
Nurseline is available on all laya healthcare schemes. We’re here for you, always, on 021 202 2861.
