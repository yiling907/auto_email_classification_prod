## Executive Health Check Centres
We believe in looking after you always. Not just when you're ill.
The Executive Health Check includes tests such as a full physical examination and consultation with a physician, chest x-ray, stress tests, a dietician consultation, if appropriate, and more.
The tests all depend on an individual’s age, history and lifestyle. These centres may change from time to time, so please contact our customer care team on 021 202 2000 prior to your health check.
Applicable members can claim from 50% of costs of an Executive Health Check towards the out-patient benefit on their policy every 2 years. Please note that not all schemes offer an Executive Health Check - you can check your cover by logging in to your Member Area or by calling our award-winning Customer Care team .
