  - Call or email us: pick up the phone and call the Laya Healthcare Concierge team, or email them directly

#### Who are the Laya Healthcare Concierges?
  - Our Laya Healthcare Concierge team are made up of experienced Nurses , who between them have decades of clinical experience under their belts, and are supported by our medical advice panel
  - Why Nurses? Nurses are the backbone of the Irish health system . They’re the ones offering friendly advice, empathy, reassurance and practical clinical guidance on what to do and where to go when it comes to a personal diagnosis or treatment. They are familiar with navigating the healthcare system and supporting patients, and so are the ideal people to form our Laya Healthcare Concierge team
  - Our dedicated Laya Healthcare Concierge team boast clinical qualifications as well as years of experience working in Ireland’s healthcare system, and they are now at the end of a telephone line or are available on email to provide reassurance and help to you.
